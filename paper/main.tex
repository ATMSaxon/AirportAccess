\section*{Reproducibility}
\label{sec:repro}
Source code, configuration files, the figure-generation scripts that produced
every plot in this paper, and the per-corridor KPI parquet artefacts are available
at \url{https://github.com/ATMSaxon/AirportAccess}. The ICAO Annex~14 parameters
that drive the geometric layer are documented in
\texttt{configs/annex14/code4\_precision.yaml}; per-airport calibration is a
single YAML override per ICAO identifier.

\section*{Data availability}
\label{sec:data-avail}
Airport geometry is derived from the FAA NASR public records; obstacles are from
the FAA Digital Obstacle File; terrain from USGS 3DEP; passenger O\&D from
BTS DB1B; ground-access demand from the LAWA 2024 Traffic Generation Report.
Historical fixed-wing trajectories come from the open
\texttt{adsb.lol} \emph{globe\_history\_2024} archive (no credentials required),
and historical METAR observations come from the Iowa State University ASOS
archive. All sources are publicly available; the orchestrator
\texttt{scripts/acquire\_all.py} retrieves them with a single command.

\section*{Acknowledgements}
\label{sec:ack}
The authors thank the volunteer ADS-B feeder community whose
\texttt{adsb.lol} data made this study possible.
